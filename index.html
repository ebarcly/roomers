Roomers

A cozy murder-mystery logic puzzle for iPhone

Every room has a story. One hides a killer.

Each case is a crime scene: a floor plan divided into rooms, furnished with tables, chairs, and secrets. Place every suspect so each row and column holds exactly one person, follow each suspect's clue, and when the board is solved — accuse. The murderer is whoever ends up alone in a room with the victim.
