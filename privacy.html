Roomers — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Roomers collects no data. Period.

What the app does

Roomers is a fully offline murder-mystery logic puzzle. Your progress, settings, and generated cases are stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to us or to anyone else — the app has no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.

Purchases

The optional one-time purchase ("Roomers Unlimited") is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Purchase records live in your Apple account and on your device.
