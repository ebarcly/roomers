Common questions

How do I restore my purchase? Settings → Roomers Unlimited → Restore Purchases. The unlock is tied to your Apple account and works on all your devices.

When does the Daily Case change? At midnight, your local time. Everyone in the world gets the same case for the same date.

I lost my streak! The streak survives until a full day passes without solving that day's case. Solving today's case revives it from 1.
